Renovation project located in Belo Horizonte, in the Nova Granada neighborhood. The project focuses on the transformation of a 40-year-old house, redefining its spatial organization through the removal of interior walls and the creation of larger openings.

The design proposes a new open-plan concept, promoting stronger visual and physical integration between the bedroom, kitchen, and outdoor areas. The intervention aims to improve natural light, circulation, and spatial continuity, updating the house to a contemporary living concept while respecting its original structure.

## Scope
- Architectural renovation
- Interior reconfiguration
- Open-plan layout design
- Integration between indoor and outdoor spaces

## Location
Belo Horizonte, Brazil
